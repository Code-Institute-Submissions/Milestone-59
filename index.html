Magic Match

How to Play

Welcome to Magic Match, a magical memory matching game!

There are 3 levels to choose from: Easy, Medium and Hard and each comes with its own challenges! Simply click to turn over the cards and get started.

Can you find all the matching pairs of magical cards before the timer runs out?
